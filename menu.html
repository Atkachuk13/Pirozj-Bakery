Menu

Desserts

  • Croissants: Flaky, buttery pastries available in various flavors such as plain, chocolate, or almond.
  • Bagels: A variety of bagels with different toppings like sesame seeds, poppy seeds, or everything bagels, served
    • with cream cheese or other spreads.
  • Cupcakes: Delicious individual-sized cakes with various frosting options and toppings.
  • Scones: Baked treats often with a crumbly texture, available in flavors like blueberry, cranberry-orange, or chocolate chip.
  • Danishes: Sweet and flaky pastries filled with fruit, cheese, or custard, and often topped with icing or sliced almonds.
  • Muffins: Moist and flavorful muffins in different flavors such as blueberry, banana nut, or lemon poppy seed.
  • Cinnamon Rolls: Rolled dough with cinnamon and sugar, baked to perfection and topped with cream cheese or icing.
  • Cookies: An assortment of cookies, including chocolate chip, oatmeal raisin, and sugar cookies, sometimes decorated for special occasions.
  • Cheesecake: Creamy and rich cheesecake with various toppings such as fruit compote, chocolate ganache, or caramel.
  • Bread Loaves: Freshly baked bread in different varieties like sourdough, whole wheat, or multigrain.

Drinks

  • Espresso: A strong, concentrated coffee brewed by forcing hot water through finely-ground coffee beans.
  • Cappuccino: A coffee drink that combines equal parts espresso, steamed milk, and milk foam.
  • Latte: Espresso mixed with steamed milk and a small amount of foam, often flavored with syrups like vanilla or caramel.
  • Americano: Espresso with added hot water, creating a coffee similar in strength to drip coffee but with a different flavor profile.
  • Chai Latte: A spiced tea beverage made with black tea, steamed milk, and a mix of aromatic spices like cinnamon, cardamom, and cloves.
  • Iced Coffee: Chilled coffee served over ice, often sweetened and/or with added milk or cream.
  • Herbal Tea: Various caffeine-free teas made from dried herbs, flowers, or fruits, such as peppermint, chamomile, or hibiscus.
  • Hot Chocolate: A warm, sweet beverage made with milk and chocolate, often topped with whipped cream or chocolate shavings.
  • Smoothies: Blended beverages made with fruits, yogurt, and ice, often offered in various flavors like berry, mango, or green smoothies.
  • Matcha Latte: A Japanese green tea beverage made with finely ground powdered green tea leaves, mixed with steamed milk.

Breakfast

  • Egg Benedict: Poached eggs served on an English muffin with fake bacon.
  • Pancakes: Fluffy stacks of pancakes served with syrup and butter, and optionally topped with fruits or nuts.
  • Omelette: A customizable dish made with beaten eggs folded around various fillings such as cheese and vegetables.
  • Avocado Toast: Sliced avocado on toasted bread, often seasoned with salt, pepper, and additional toppings like poached eggs or cherry tomatoes.
  • Breakfast Burrito: A tortilla filled with scrambled eggs, cheese, and vegetables.
  • French Toast: Slices of bread soaked in a mixture of beaten eggs and milk, then cooked until golden brown and served with syrup and powdered sugar.
  • Greek Yogurt Parfait: Layers of Greek yogurt, granola, and fresh fruits, often drizzled with honey or maple syrup.
  • Bagel with Smoked Salmon: A toasted bagel topped with cream cheese, smoked salmon, capers, and red onion slices.
  • Breakfast Sandwich: A sandwich typically made with eggs or cheese, served on a bagel, English muffin, or croissant.
  • Granola Bowl: A bowl filled with granola, yogurt, and a variety of toppings like nuts, seeds, and berries.
